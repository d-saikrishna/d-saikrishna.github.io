# How does a girl fall for a guy?

This post is for guys only!

**How does a girl fall for a guy?**

Many movies showed us that its generally a fight. The guy hits some eve-teasers and damaal — the girl stares at him in slow motion. Or the guy himself is an eve-teaser and in some stockholm syndromish way, the girl falls for the guy.

One way to unlearn this is to listen from women directly about what they like in men. I’ve had that privilege. Otherwise, read romance novels by women authors.

I read this beautiful romance novel - Book Lovers by Emily Henry

Not once did the author mention the bicep curl of Charlie, the male protagonist. Or his ‘alphaness’. She wrote more about his smiles, pouts, smirks and humour. And the way he stood for the heroine when needed. 

This isn’t to dishonestly tuning ourselves the way women want. We are already out-of-tune listening to incels. We can liberate ourselves from all those burdening expectations by knowing what women actually want. Go listen, or read!
