# What is Literature?

I came across a definition of literature in a course taught by Prof. Chintakindi Kasim. English definition of that definition would be:
`
Literature is the presentation of natural truths in a beautiful way. 
`

The focus is on two words here: *truth* and *beautiful*

In the book "Entangled Life", I found an example that illustrates this definition well. It is the story of Ninkasi.

About 4000 years ago, people of Mesopotamia (~Iraq) discovered the magic of brewing beer. The liquid made them high. Failing to reason it out, they did the obvious- they created a goddess out if it: Ninkasi, the goddess of beer and brewing.
