# Assign a purpose to your notebooks

<div style="text-align: center">  <img src="blogs/singlepurposenotebooks.jpg" style="width: 100%; max-width: 500px;" /></div>

I got a lot of notebooks as gifts. Some of them are so cute that I cannot use them for running notes. So, I assign a project to each of them. I write poetry in one. I learn Kannada in another. Recently, I got the tiniest of the lot and I’m drawing tiny sketches in it.

Cal Newport, author of Slow Productivity and Deep Work, validates this habit of mine😛.

TLDR:
Assign a purpose to a notebook. Every project of yours should have a notebook and you carry that with you instead of your phone during ideation. The book will remain as an artifact of your thinking for that particular project.
